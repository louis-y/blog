Hi! My name is Charlotte Johnson, and I’m a fashion blogger. If you are a bit of a fashionista, you are in the right place. I grew up in San Francisco. At a very young age, I noticed that my mother was obsessed with makeup and fashion.

She had dozens of dresses and hundreds of shoes, and I loved going through all of them and playing dress-up.
